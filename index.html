About

A young builder of brands, books, worlds, and strange little sparks.

I’m Sergio Fernandes — also ColourXD — a Goa-based creator working between web design, branding, writing, school projects, visual identities, and experimental digital ideas. I like things that feel handmade, alive, slightly impossible, and still useful enough to convert a visitor into a client.

Fuelerz is where the business side burns. ColourlessDreams is where the atmosphere softens. SpecialAutopsy is where the darker, sharper work gets dissected. ColourXD is the player tag running through all of it.
